About Us

Quality sounds was founded in 1943 by a young man who had an interest in music. Bob Smith was born in 1920 in the United Kingdom, he always had an interest in music and business, therefore he was determined to create a company which allowed him to sell music globally.

Quality sounds started of small only selling locally. However, it quickly grew! Bob Smith would sell vinyls and posters to customers in different cities. By 1950 Quality Sounds was a the number 1 business in the music industry for selling and distribuitng music around the UK. in the 1960s Quality sounds reached the international market

After reaching the international markets; quality sounds became what it is today, a multi-million pound company. the Company is now run by Bob Smith's children and the company is more prosperous than ever before.
